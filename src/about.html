Hey there, I'm Michelle!

I'm a product designer and front-end developer based in Los Angeles with a B.A. in Sociology from UC Irvine and a UX Design Career certificate from Springboard. With 5 years of experience in digital marketing, a business acumen, and 2 years of experience in service design, I create innovative, scalable, and resilient user experiences. Given my front-end skills, I bridge the gap between design and development teams, ensuring smooth handoffs.

My journey began with graphic design and marketing during college, where I developed a strong empathy for users. This led me to self-teach coding and independently revamp the UCI Basic Needs Center website in 2022, enhancing access and productivity. This experience ignited my passion for product design and full-time pursuit!

I am currently available for freelance projects and seeking a full-time position in Product Design, UX Design, UX research, or web development. If you'd like to collaborate or chat, shoot me an email, connect with me on LinkedIn, or both!
